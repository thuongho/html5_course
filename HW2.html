Personal Responsibility

The name of the game is personal responsibility. That means that you just need to take care of yourself and not care about things like community involvement, poor people, or the obscene gap between the rich and poor in this country.

Ever wonder why the sky is blue? The sun's ray is made up of every color. However, when it hits the Earth's atmosphere, the sunlight is scattered in all directions by all the gases and particles in the air. Since blue has the shortest wavelength, that color is scattered more than other colors. Thus, the sky is blue most of the time. Reference from NASA.
